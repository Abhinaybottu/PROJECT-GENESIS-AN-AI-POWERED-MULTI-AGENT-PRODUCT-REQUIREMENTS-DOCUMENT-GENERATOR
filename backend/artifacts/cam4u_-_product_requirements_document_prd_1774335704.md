# Cam4U - Product Requirements Document

## 1. Introduction
Cam4U is an innovative project focused on developing an AI-Powered Real-time Surveillance and Anomaly Detection system. This system aims to enhance security and monitoring capabilities by intelligently identifying unusual activities and events in real-time, significantly improving response times and operational efficiency.

## 2. Project Goals
The primary goal of Cam4U is to deliver a robust, intelligent surveillance solution capable of:
*   Providing real-time video surveillance.
*   Utilizing AI models for accurate anomaly detection.
*   Reducing human effort in continuous monitoring.
*   Improving security and safety across various environments.

## 3. Key Features
*   **Real-time Video Processing**: Continuous monitoring and analysis of live video feeds.
*   **AI-Powered Anomaly Detection**: Sophisticated AI models to identify unusual patterns, behaviors, or events that deviate from normal activity.
*   **Alerting System**: Instant notifications upon detection of anomalies to relevant personnel.
*   **Scalable Architecture**: Designed to integrate with various surveillance infrastructures.
*   **Data Collection & Training**: Processes for gathering and labeling surveillance footage to train and refine AI models.

## 4. Target Audience
*   Security agencies and firms
*   Commercial and industrial enterprises
*   Public sector organizations
*   Facility management companies
*   Anyone requiring advanced surveillance and security monitoring solutions.

## 5. Project Phases and Roadmap

The project will be executed in the following phases:

### Phase 1: Research and Planning
*   **Duration**: 30 days
*   **Description**: Conduct comprehensive market research to understand existing solutions and identify gaps. Define the detailed project scope, technical requirements, and create a comprehensive project plan, including resource allocation and timelines.

### Phase 2: Data Collection and Labeling
*   **Duration**: 60 days
*   **Description**: Acquire a diverse dataset of surveillance footage. This includes both normal operational footage and various types of anomalous events. The collected data will be meticulously labeled to train and validate AI models effectively.

### Phase 3: AI Model Development
*   **Duration**: 90 days
*   **Description**: Design, develop, and train advanced AI models specifically tailored for real-time surveillance and anomaly detection. This phase involves algorithm selection, model architecture design, hyperparameter tuning, and rigorous testing of model performance and accuracy.

### Phase 4: System Integration and Testing
*   **Duration**: 60 days
*   **Description**: Integrate the developed AI models seamlessly with existing or new surveillance hardware and software systems. Conduct extensive system-level testing, including functional testing, performance testing, security testing, and user acceptance testing (UAT) to ensure robustness and reliability.

### Phase 5: Deployment and Maintenance
*   **Duration**: 30 days
*   **Description**: Deploy the Cam4U system in target environments. Provide comprehensive training and support to end-users and administrators. Establish a framework for ongoing maintenance, performance monitoring, and regular software updates to ensure continuous optimal operation.

## 6. Technical Architecture (High-Level)
Cam4U will comprise:
*   **Edge Devices/Cameras**: For video capture.
*   **Data Ingestion Layer**: To handle real-time video streams.
*   **AI Processing Unit**: Dedicated hardware/software for running anomaly detection models.
*   **Cloud/On-premise Infrastructure**: For data storage, model training, and further analysis.
*   **User Interface**: For monitoring, alerts, and system configuration.

## 7. Success Metrics
*   **Anomaly Detection Accuracy**: High precision and recall rates for identified anomalies.
*   **False Positive Rate**: Minimizing false alerts to prevent alert fatigue.
*   **Real-time Processing Capability**: Low latency in anomaly detection and alerting.
*   **System Uptime and Reliability**: Ensuring continuous operation of the surveillance system.
*   **User Satisfaction**: Feedback from security personnel and system administrators.
*   **Deployment Efficiency**: Ease and speed of integration with various surveillance setups.
